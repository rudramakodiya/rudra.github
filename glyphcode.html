GlyphCode

I invented a new writing style known as GlyphCode. GlyphCode is a self-created symbolic writing system.

In this writing style, letters and words of the English language are represented using unique symbols. It reflects creativity, originality, and structured thinking.

GlyphCode uses symbols such as @ | ₹ ¶ ∆ and many more. I have completed symbols for all alphabets from A to Z.

It may look confusing to others, but it is meaningful and systematic.
